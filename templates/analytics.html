Analytics

Heatmap

The heatmap gives you an insight in the "hot" zones in your restoration areas. It is calculated over a period of a month and represents the distribution of orders dependent on the tables. An even distribution - that is, a flat heatmap - means that costumers don't have preferences when choosing tables and this facilitates a good flow within the restaurant.
